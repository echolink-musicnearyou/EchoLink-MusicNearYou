Delete your EchoLink account

This page permanently erases your EchoLink account and the data associated with it. Sign in below to confirm it's really you, then confirm the deletion.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (display name, email, avatar)
  • Your friendships and blocks
  • Your location and music listening history
  • Your message requests and conversation memberships
  • Your login credentials

What stays

  • Messages you sent to other people remain in their inboxes, but appear as from a deleted user.

This cannot be undone. If you'd rather have us delete your data manually, email contact@echolink.app and we'll erase it within 30 days.
